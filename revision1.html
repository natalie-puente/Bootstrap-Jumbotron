My Hobbies

Shopping - During my free time, I like to go shopping and spend money that I don't have! At stores, I like to shop for new shoes and T-shirts. My favorite clothing stores include: Ross, Target, and the mall. However, my favorite stores to shop for shoes are the Converse store and the New Balance store. I especially love to shop, when I have friends with me, but whenever they come with me, I usually end up spending more than I should have. Shopping and clothes are my biggest weakness!

Community Service - I also like to participate in community service. I love to travel around town helping others, whenever they need me. Usually, one will see me helping out at our local WRAP organization. I can also be found at our local park, helping kids learn the game of soccer. On Tuesday evenings and Saturday mornings, I am always at the park teaching kids the rules and techniques correlating to soccer.

My Favorite Sports

Sports - Since I was little, my favorite sport was soccer. I used to play all the time and on many different teams. On T.V., I like to watch the U.S. National Women's team play soccer. They are one of my favorite teams in the national soccer league. One time, I even got to see the team play in Nashville at Nissan Stadium. That was the best experience I have ever had!

While I do like to play soccer, I also love to watch football. During the months of September through early February, I like to watch NFL football, especially on Sundays. One thing not many people know about is that my favorite football team is the New England Patriots, but I like to watch all the teams.
